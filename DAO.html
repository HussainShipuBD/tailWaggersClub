A decentralized autonomous organization (DAO), is an organization represented by rules encoded as a computer program that is transparent, controlled by the organization members and not influenced by a central government.

50% of the mint and secondary royalties go into the Tail Waggers Club wallet , a governance DAO for the community. After successful mint, there will be over 500ETH inside.

Holding a Tail Waggers Club NFT grants you access to the exclusive DAO community, including voting rights over fractionalized assets, as well as future purchases. All the voting will be made in Snapshoot. Holders also receive perks such as P2E game control, claimable NFTs, airdrops and much more!

The implementation of a community fund will also allows for future marketing expenses to be covered, which means that the project will still garner attention from the public even after the collection is sold out.

In our early stages, the contract will be more controlled and easily updated. In later stages, the control over the contract will be incrementally released at an increasing rate until 100% decentralization is reached.
