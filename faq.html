  • What is DAO?

    The Tail Waggers Club was born after we saw a lot of failed NFT projects where community members didn’t have any influence on the decision making. Our mission is to create a motivated, NFT loving DAO community. TWC is for people who want to be a part of a project that is focused on becoming a big player in the metaverse space and build a strong community. After the mint, we will transfer 50% of the funds into a community wallet. Those funds will be used to finish the development of our P2E game, investing in land plots in the metaverse and collaborate with large NFT projects.
